🧠 AGE CALCULATOR WITH HEALTH AI 🩺
AGE-CALCULATOR-WITH-HEALTH-AI is a smart web-based tool that not only calculates your exact age in years, months, and days but also integrates a basic Health AI assistant that offers personalized health tips based on your age group. It's designed to provide both useful and interactive insights in a single platform.

🔧 Features
📅 Accurate age calculator (based on DOB)

🧠 Health AI logic that gives:

Fitness suggestions

Diet tips

General wellness advice

🖥️ Clean, responsive user interface
